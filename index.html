From Birth Until Now

What Have You Done?

Think-Pair-Share: Share your own life experiences.

When Did It Happen?

  • Unspecified time before now
  • Focus on the experience, not when it happened
  • Contrast with Simple Past: I visited Brazil last year. (Specific time)
  • I have visited Brazil. (Unspecified time, experience)

How to Make It

  • Subject + have/has + Past Participle (V3)
  • Examples: I have eaten sushi. She has seen that movie.
  • Negative: Subject + have/has + not + Past Participle.
  • Question: Have/Has + Subject + Past Participle...?

Tricky Past Participles

  • be - been
  • see - seen
  • eat - eaten
  • do - done
  • go - gone/been
  • write - written
  • take - taken
  • make - made
  • buy - bought
  • read - read /riːd/

Say It Right!

  • 'have' /həv/ (weak form)
  • 'has' /həz/ (weak form)
  • Contractions: I haven't /aɪ ˈhævnt/, She hasn't /ʃi ˈhæznt/

Complete the Gaps

  • I ________ (visit) Paris twice.
  • She _______ never ________ (try) Indian food.

Answers

  • I have visited Paris twice.
  • She has never tried Indian food.

Change the Tense!

  • I saw that movie last week. → I have seen that movie.

Find Someone Who Has...

Stand up, grab your bingo card, and find out who has done these things. Use the Present Perfect!

How to Play

  • Walk around and ask classmates: 'Have you ever...?'
  • If 'Yes, I have,' write their name in the square.
  • If 'No, I haven\'t,' move to another person.
  • Model Dialogue:
    • S1: 'Have you ever visited another continent?'
    • S2: 'Yes, I have.' (S1 writes S2's name) OR 'No, I haven\'t.'
